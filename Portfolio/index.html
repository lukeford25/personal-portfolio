About Me

Luke Ford is an aspiring cybersecurity professional currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at Union Adventist University. With a strong foundation in business, evidenced by his Bachelor's in Business Administration, Luke brings a unique blend of business acumen and technical skills to the table.

Currently working in Information Systems as a desktop support specialist, Luke is gaining hands-on experience in IT infrastructure and user support. This role fuels his passion for cybersecurity, as he witnesses firsthand the importance of robust security measures in today's digital landscape.

Luke's technical repertoire includes proficiency in Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. He has demonstrated his coding skills through various projects, including a Python-based Pokémon battle simulator and several React Native applications. These projects showcase his ability to create interactive and engaging software solutions.

As he works towards his CompTIA A+ certification, Luke is dedicated to expanding his knowledge and staying current with the latest trends in technology and security. His goal is to transition into a cybersecurity role, where he can leverage his diverse skill set to protect digital assets and contribute to the ever-evolving field of information security.

Luke is passionate about the intersection of technology and privacy, and is eager to explore innovative ways to safeguard digital information in an increasingly connected world.
